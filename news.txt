US-Iran Peace Agreement Announced
- Peace Deal Announced: Pakistan’s Prime Minister Shehbaz Sharif announced that the United States and Iran have reached a peace agreement to permanently end military operations on all fronts, including Lebanon. An official signing ceremony is scheduled for June 19 in Switzerland.
- Terms and Conditions: Reports indicate the framework includes a ceasefire, the lifting of the US naval blockade, the reopening of the Strait of Hormuz, and the release of $24 billion in frozen Iranian assets.
- Trump’s Warning: President Donald Trump stated that while the agreement is expected to be signed Friday, he would resume military strikes against Tehran if a final nuclear accord is not reached. He also reportedly warned that he would make the US the "guardian of the Middle East" in exchange for 20% of the region's revenues if the nuclear deal fails.
